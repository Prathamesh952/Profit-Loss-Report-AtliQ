# Profit-Loss-Report-AtliQ
Profit &amp; Loss Report of AtliQ

Profit & Loss Report of AtliQ
This repository contains consolidated financial and sales performance reports for AtliQ, aimed at providing transparency, insight, and decision-support for stakeholders.

Included reports (PDFs):

Customer Performance Report — shows revenue/volume per customer and helps identify key customers.

Market Performance vs Target Report — compares actual performance across markets against targets; highlights strong and under-performing markets.

Profit & Loss (P&L) Statement by Fiscal Year — long-term view of revenue, costs, and profit per fiscal year.

P&L Statement by Months — monthly P&L for analyzing seasonality and short-term trends.

P&L Statement by Markets — profitability broken down by market or region, useful for strategic planning and resource allocation.

Value & Insights Gained:

Identify top-performing customers, markets, and channels.

Monitor profitability trends over time (monthly & annually).

Assess market-wise profitability and growth potential.

Support budgeting, forecasting, and business decisions with data-driven analytics.

Possible Workflow / Tools:
Data ingestion → cleaning (ETL) → transformation (date table, grouping by customer/market/time) → aggregation → export as PDF reports.

Usage:
These reports are designed for internal stakeholders — management, finance team, sales team — to make strategic decisions, manage costs, plan expansion, and assess profitability across customers and markets.
